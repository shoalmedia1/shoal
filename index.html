what

ShoalMedia (or simply ‘Shoal’) is a community celebrating all things Film, TV & Popular Culture. Made up of a small team, we host The Shoal Weekly Podcast every Monday on YouTube, Spotify & Google Podcasts, where we talk about the latest in Film, the greatest in TV and all Pop-Culture in between.

Once a season, Shoal is always proud to present an Online Film Festival, where we curate an exclusively themed programme of Motion Pictures exploring some of the best corners of Cinematic History. In fact, Shoal’s first project to mark its birth was the Festival “Time and Moments in Passing”, celebrating Films that expressed the beauty of Time, Memory & Identity. You can look forward to our future festivals by following us at our Social Media pages on Twitter, Facebook & Instagram, for all Festival and Weekly Podcast News.
