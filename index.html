upcoming

what

Shoal’s first official event, to mark the birth of our online community, will be a virtual film festival. With a combination of Watch Parties, Live Podcasts and other exclusive events, Shoal is dedicated to curating programmes that show a wide variety of representations of various themes in cinema.

The first virtual film festival will be themed around Time and Moments in Passing (learn more about it here). Stay tuned for more events in 2021, as we build our community and continue to broaden the conversation about film.

when

Our first virtual festival will run from 1st December through to 24th December. Each film in the calendar will be announced 24 hours prior on our Twitter. We are hosting various events throughout the festival, including discussions on our Twitch, virtual screenings on Kast and watch-alongs. Be sure to add the dates to your diary and come join the conversation.

We’re very excited to get this project started and hope to see many of you join our community over the coming weeks.
